Audio quality

Audio formats used by Audio Downloader:

  • WebM (Opus) unprocessed raw audio
  • Opus unprocessed raw audio
  • AAC (m4a) raw audio
  • Vorbis unprocessed raw audio
  • FLAC lossless compression
  • Mp3 VBR lossy compression
